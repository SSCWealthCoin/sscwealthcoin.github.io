About SSC Wealth Coin

SSC Wealth Coin (SSCWC) is a cryptocurrency backed by gold bullion, designed to be a stable digital asset by linking its value to gold prices. It operates as a BEP-20 token on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and is tradable on decentralized exchanges like DEXTswap.

The project’s white paper is available on Scribd. The official contract address on the BNB Chain is: 0xd7deadbf768dec8ac13850e4f6787ac53a9d0447

Key Features

  • Gold-backed: Stability supported by physical gold reserves.
  • Volatility Hedge: Designed to protect against crypto market fluctuations.
  • Secure Storage: Assets stored in certified vaults with Krugerrand verification.
  • BEP-20 Token: Runs on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) for fast and low-cost transactions.
  • Trading: Available via SSCWC/WBNB pair on DEXTswap.
